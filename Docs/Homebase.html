The Homebase

The Homebase
Location: Veilstone City

The player will own their own place in Veilstone City, provided by Fortis. Lark introduces the player to this place at the start of the game. By interacting with the panel, different kinds of furniture and wall/floor styles can be bought, in exchange for different currencies, including Season Coins. After making purchases, the player can use the Panel functions to place, move and withdraw furniture in the house.

Background Music

Vinyls can be obtained by winning Tournaments at the Pristine Lounge. They will not show up in the Bag. On the contrary, the will appear as a new selectable song from the Stereo, provided the Player has bought it. Once selected, the Stereo sets the background music of the Homebase as the selected track. They cannot be sold.
