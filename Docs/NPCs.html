Useful NPCs

Merchants of the Four Seasons

Location: Celestic Town; New Hearthome City; Floaroma Town; Rockfrost Point. Purpose: Several tasks related to Season Coins.

Move Tutor

Location: Lustrous Academy in Celestic Town. Purpose: Teaches a new or forgotten move, or deletes one.

Peer Tutor

Location: Pokémon Day-Care in Eterna City. Purpose: Teaches Egg Moves.

>Master Traitsu

Location: Snowpoint Temple. Purpose: Swaps the Ability of a Pokémon.

Dr. Basalt, Archaeologist

Location: Route 207. Purpose: Revives Fossils. Pre-requirement: Lead Mira out of Wayward Cave.

Name Rater

Location: Galerie Pradau. Purpose: Changes Pokémon Nickname.

Collectors

Location: Several. Purpose: They buy certain Items at a high price.
