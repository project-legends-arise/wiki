Calypsolens

The Calypsolens is an electronic device given to the player character by Agent Fortis at the start of the game. It appears as a small tablet with a dark, grid-lined display and a circular interface centered on a black dial, with several colored icons arranged around it.

Origin

The Calypsolens is part of the Calypso Technology series developed by Agent Fortis. This model is the original prototype he built with his brother, Volkner, during their adolescence.

It was designed as a human-support exploration tool: an artificial enhancement device meant to help users traverse difficult terrain, locate resources, and stay in contact over long distances.

The Calypsoscope worn by Fortis is an upgraded, visor-mounted version of the same technology.

Main Functions

The Calypsolens provides several core functions throughout the adventure:

  • Town Map: Displays the map of the Sinnoh region and the player character's current location.
  • Itemfinder: Detects hidden items in the overworld.
  • Achievements: Stores collected Medals and allows the player to re-read Platinum Berlitz's journal entries.
  • Interaction Tool: Enables context-specific overworld actions.

Additional utility features include:

  • Running Shoes Toggle: Lets the player choose whether to keep Running Shoes active.
  • Assigned Key Item: Uses the currently assigned Key Item.
  • Alerts: Delivers messages from Agent Fortis and notifications for environmental conditions, especially Spacetime Distortions.
  • Summary: Selecting the central geometric icon opens a panel that tracks upgrades to the Interaction Tool.

Interaction Tool

The Interaction Tool is used to influence the environment and gather information. Depending on the object or situation in front of the player, the normally blank display fills with a specific icon representing the available action.

  • Lockpicker: Opens electronic doors, sometimes with password support, and works in synergy with the Jammer. Story use: opens the Galaxias Labs secret door in the Veilstain during Chapter 1.
  • Signal Enhancer: Amplifies faint sound waves to eavesdrop on distant or muffled conversations. Story use: boosts the conversation between Mars, Jupiter, and Galaxias Technicians in Chapter 1.
  • Jammer: Emits disruptive electronic waves to disable selected technology. Story uses: disables closed-circuit cameras in the Red Chain Engine Room (Chapter 1) and removes the Psyduck blocking the Eastern Hearthome Gate (Chapter 3).
  • Decrypter: Translates messages sent through electromagnetic waves and can decode ancient or cryptic text with sufficient training data. Story use: decrypts Fortis's message in the Production Control Hub in Chapter 1.

Special Functions

  • Teleporter: Teleports the player character to the Secret Hideout. It is used in Chapter 5 to escape the Old Sanatorium.
  • Striker: Emits a powerful electromagnetic wave that fries the Calypsoscope and interrupts the climax at Spear Pillar.
