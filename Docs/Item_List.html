Item Documentation

Custom Items

Season Coins

Season Coins are an important part of gameplay. They can be found in the environment through the Phenomena mechanic (sparkling grass/glittering digging point) and can be traded for goods and services. One important role they have is as currency for Move Tutors or Furniture. Four Merchants, each representing a season, are also present around the region, asking for tasks and giving coins as rewards.

Candies

Exp. Candies

In addition to being available for purchase, an Exp. Candy will appear as extra support after a Deja-vu Sequence.

Contest Candies

These are sold at the Candy Store in New Hearthome City, they serve to increase Contest stats just like Poffins.

Puffs

Snacks

Fragrances

These are sold at the Galaxias Store in New Hearthome City and serve to increase the Friendship stat of a Pokémon.

EV-reducing Tablets

Relics

Relics can be found in Type-IV Mining Spots and when gifted to John Meerou they allow the player to access several pocket dimensions in which Legendary and Mythical Pokémon from past generations can be caught.
