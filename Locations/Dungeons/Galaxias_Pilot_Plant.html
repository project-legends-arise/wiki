Description

This article covers this location as it appears during the main campaign. For a detailed history and lore, please visit the following article: The Birth of Galaxias Novus.

The Pilot Plant is an industrial facility operated by Galaxias Novus, built for experimental production and chemical processing. The interior is paved with blue-grey hexagonal tiles, and the metal-panelled walls are lined with exposed pipes and utility fixtures, giving the space a cold, mechanical feel suited to its function. The floor is cluttered with wooden crates, barrels, and shipping containers, many of them stacked haphazardly or left half-open. Packaging materials and loose items lie scattered across the area, with workstations often buried under disorganized supplies. The facility is notoriously poorly maintained by its staff, who frequently face reprimands from Galaxias higher-ups for the general disorder and lack of care.
